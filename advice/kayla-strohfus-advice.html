Hi, my name is Kayla and I have some advice for you!

Don't Give Up!

When you are learning to code and you think you're out of your league. Throw that thought in the trash and burn it! You got this!

When things get tough, take a break and come back to it later!

Practice, Practice, and Practice Some More!

Pratice makes perfect as they say. When learning something new i.e. to code, you'll want to keep practicing techniques and skills to make it stick!

Daily practice of a new skill will make it not so new when it's done daily!
